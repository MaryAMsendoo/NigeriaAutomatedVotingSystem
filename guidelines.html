Rules and Guidlines

    To ensure a smooth and fair election process, please adhere to the following rules:

  • Each voter is only allowed to voter once. Voting multiple times is strictly prohibited.

  • All votes are private and secure. Your personl information will not be share with anyone.

  • Only registered citizens with valid voter IDs are eligible to participate in the election.

  • All votes must be cast before the official election deadline. Any votes submitted after this period will not be counted.
